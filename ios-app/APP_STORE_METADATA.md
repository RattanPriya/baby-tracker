## Promotional Text

Track feeds, bottles, sleep, diapers, medicine, growth, activities, reminders, and gentle age-aware insights in one calm local-first baby log.

## Description

Baby Tracker helps tired parents keep a clear record of newborn care without extra noise. Log nursing, bottles, pumping, sleep, diapers, medicine, growth, and activities in seconds. Review the daily timeline, see simple summaries, turn on optional local reminders, and export logs when you want to share information with a caregiver or clinician.

Baby Tracker also includes gentle week-by-week guidance written in original language from reputable pediatric and infant-care sources. Guidance is designed to help you notice patterns, prepare questions, and feel more organized without pressure or judgment.

The app is designed for fast one-handed use, night-friendly tracking, and privacy-first storage. Core baby data stays on your device. Exports are created only when you choose to share a CSV or PDF.

Baby Tracker Plus is optional. Core care logging stays free. Plus unlocks personalized weekly insights tied to your logs, advanced 7-day and 30-day patterns, pediatrician prep reports, saved guidance, parent check-ins, calm care rhythm badges, and caregiver handoff tools. Launch pricing: $4.99/month with a 14-day free trial. Subscription renews automatically unless canceled in App Store account settings.

Baby Tracker is a personal logging tool. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical advice. Contact your pediatrician or local emergency services with medical concerns.

## Review Notes

Baby Tracker is a local-first wellness and recordkeeping app for parent-entered baby care logs. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, dosing advice, emergency triage, sleep training plans, or medical recommendations.

Optional local notifications are scheduled only when the user enables a reminder. CSV and PDF exports are generated only after an intentional user action through the system share sheet.

The app uses Apple in-app purchases for the optional Baby Tracker Plus subscription. The launch product is `baby_tracker_plus_monthly`, mapped to the RevenueCat entitlement `baby_tracker_plus`. A restore purchases control is available in Settings. Core logging remains available without a subscription.
