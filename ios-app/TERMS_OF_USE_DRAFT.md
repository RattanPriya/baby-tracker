# Terms of Use Draft

Effective date: replace before release.

These draft terms are a product placeholder and should be reviewed by counsel before App Store submission.

## Baby Tracker

Baby Tracker is a personal wellness and recordkeeping app for parent-entered baby care logs. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, dosing advice, emergency triage, or professional medical advice.

Contact your pediatrician or emergency services for urgent concerns.

## Baby Tracker Plus

Baby Tracker Plus is an optional auto-renewing subscription that unlocks personalized insights, advanced care patterns, saved guidance, pediatrician prep reports, parent check-ins, calm progress badges, and caregiver handoff tools.

Launch offer:

- Price: $4.99 per month
- Free trial: 14 days
- Product ID: `baby_tracker_plus_monthly`
- Entitlement: `baby_tracker_plus`

Payment is charged to the Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. The subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in App Store account settings.

Core care logging remains available without a Baby Tracker Plus subscription.

## User Data

You are responsible for the information you enter into the app. Core baby care data is designed to stay local to your device unless you choose to export or share it.

## No Guarantees

Baby development varies. Insights, summaries, and reports are educational and organizational only. They should not be used as the sole basis for health decisions.
