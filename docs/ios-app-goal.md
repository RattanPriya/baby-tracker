# iOS App Goal

Turn the baby tracker into a polished, privacy-first iOS app that can be published on the App Store.

## Product Goal

Build an original baby tracking app for new parents that makes logging newborn care fast, calm, and reliable. The app helps parents track feeds, bottles, pumping, sleep, diapers, medicine, growth, activities, reminders, and daily patterns without feeling overwhelming.

## MVP Scope

- Native iOS app experience
- Baby profile with name, birth date, and optional photo
- Multiple baby support
- One-tap logging for nursing, bottle, sleep, diaper, pump, medicine, growth, and activity
- Detailed log forms for duration, side, amount, notes, time editing, and deletion
- Daily timeline and summary cards
- 7-day and 30-day trends
- Reminders and notifications
- Local-first storage
- Export-ready data architecture for CSV/PDF
- Onboarding, settings, privacy policy-ready data model

## Quality Bar

- Common logs take under 5 seconds
- Works offline
- One-handed use at night
- No diagnosis or unsafe medical claims
- Clear clinician escalation language
- Accessible text sizes, contrast, VoiceOver labels, and large tap targets
- Original branding, UI, and copy
