# App Store Readiness Checklist

## Product Safety

- No medical diagnosis, treatment plan, or guaranteed sleep outcome claims.
- Every health-adjacent guidance surface includes clinician escalation language where appropriate.
- Emergency wording is conservative and pediatrician-oriented.

## Privacy

- MVP stores baby and care data locally on device.
- Privacy policy states what is collected, where it is stored, and how exports work.
- Notifications are optional and explain their purpose before permission.
- No analytics, ads, third-party SDKs, or remote sync until explicitly designed and disclosed.

## Accessibility

- 44pt minimum tap targets.
- Dynamic Type-ready layouts.
- VoiceOver labels for quick logging, deletion, date controls, and summaries.
- Dark mode and night use verified.
- Color is not the only indicator of event type.

## Release

- Replace placeholder bundle ID.
- Add final app icon and splash screen.
- Add privacy policy URL and support URL.
- Prepare App Store screenshots for 6.7", 6.5", and 5.5" display classes.
- Test offline use, app relaunch persistence, notification permissions, and export flows.
